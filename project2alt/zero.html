1922

De Stijl made its voice
all over Europe. Month
by month the periodical spread the principles
of elemental creativity. Theo Van Doesburg
travel from city to city delivering lectures.
'The progressive architects from Holland have
adopted an international standpoint', which 'has grown up to practice'.
The field of practice
has itself expanded.
In 1920 DeStijl formulated
its literature manifesto
and in 1921 the manifesto 'Vers une nouvelle formation du monde'.
At the International
Artist Congress in
Dusseldorf in May,
Van Doesburg announced:
'We are preparing
the way for the use of an objective universal means of creation'.
