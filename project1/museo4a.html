Figure and Form: Recent Acquisitions to the Permanent Collection

This exhibition features a selection of recent acquisitions to El Museo’s permanent collection.



It includes our most recent gift, a large painting by David Antonio Cruz, Puerto Rican Pieta, a portrait of the artist and his mother.



Works of photography, prints,
sculpture, and ceramics accompany paintings in this multi-media exhibition that highlights the iconic presence of the human form in various ways. Quiet moments of (self) reflection are seen as are individual statements about the possibilities of a particular material, such as wood, ceramics, or the photograph.
